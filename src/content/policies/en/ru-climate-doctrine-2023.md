The updated 2023 Climate Doctrine of the Russian Federation reaffirms carbon neutrality by 2060 and prioritizes CCUS development. It is supported by mandatory carbon reporting for large emitters (starting 2023) and the Sakhalin Carbon Experiment, which aims for regional neutrality by 2025 through a quota-based system and domestic technological sovereignty.
