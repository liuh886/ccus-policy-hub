# Project Background

The CCUS Policy Hub was established to provide a comprehensive, structured, and accessible database of Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage (CCUS) policies worldwide.

## Our Mission

To accelerate the deployment of CCUS technologies by providing policy intelligence and facilitating data-driven decision-making for stakeholders in the energy and industrial sectors.
