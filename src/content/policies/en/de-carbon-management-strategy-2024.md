The 2024 Carbon Management Strategy (CMS) and the KSpG amendment mark Germany's pivotal shift towards proactive CCUS implementation. It permits offshore storage in the EEZ, grants federal states 'opt-in' powers for onshore storage, and defines CCUS as being in the 'overriding public interest' to accelerate permitting. Coal-fired power is excluded.
