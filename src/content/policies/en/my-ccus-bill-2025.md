The Malaysia CCUS Bill 2025 provides a comprehensive regulatory framework for the entire CCUS value chain. It establishes MyCCUS as a one-stop agency and introduces a Post-Closure Stewardship Fund via an injection levy. The bill permits CO2 import for storage but prohibits utilization of imported CO2.
