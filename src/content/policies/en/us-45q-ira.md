## Overview
The Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) significantly enhanced 45Q credits, raising sequestration value to $85/t and introducing direct pay mechanisms, anchoring the global CCUS value chain.

## Key Specifications
- **Implementation Year**: 2022
- **Category**: Incentive
- **Pore Space Rights**: Primarily private ownership (varies by state)
- **Liability Transfer**: Long-term stewardship via Class VI permit framework
- **Cross-border Rules**: Allowed via bilateral arrangements; aligns with London Protocol intent
- **CO2 Definition**: Managed substance/Commodity
- **Permitting Lead Time**: 3-6 years (Class VI timeline)
- **Incentive Duration**: 12 years
