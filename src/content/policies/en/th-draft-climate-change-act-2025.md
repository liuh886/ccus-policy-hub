Thailand's draft Climate Change Act, approved in Dec 2025, establishes a centralized governance framework under the National Climate Change Policy Committee (NCCPC). It introduces a preliminary carbon tax (THB 200/ton) and designates the Department of Mineral Fuels (DMF) as the lead for CCS, focusing on the Upper Gulf of Thailand.
