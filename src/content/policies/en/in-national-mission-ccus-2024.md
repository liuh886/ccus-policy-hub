The 2024 National Mission for CCUS, led by NITI Aayog, establishes a comprehensive framework including Viability Gap Funding (VGF), regional hub development, and a regulatory roadmap for pore space and liability. It integrates with the Indian Carbon Market (CCTS) to monetize emissions reductions.
